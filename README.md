## What Makes a Great Espresso?

- **Freshly roasted beans** — ideally within 2-4 weeks of roasting
- **Proper grind size** — fine and consistent
- **Water temperature** — between 195°F and 205°F
- **Pressure** — 9 bars of pressure for optimal extraction
- **Timing** — 25-30 seconds for a standard double shot
